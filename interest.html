Jogging

I like to jog because it helps me decompress and stay in shape. I get to start my day full of energy and positivity thanks to it. As I run, I enjoy the sensation of the clean air and the cadence of my footsteps. I only go brisk walking or running when I have free time. I go out with my friends occasionally and alone other times. In addition, regular jogging helps students maintain a healthy weight, strengthen their cardiovascular systems, and increase their endurance, all of which will benefit my physical health. Because jogging promotes the release of endorphins, which can lessen stress, anxiety, and depressive symptoms, it also improves mental health. It also elevates mood in general.
